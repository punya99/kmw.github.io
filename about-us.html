Krishna Metal Works is one of the leading manufacturers of Copper and Copper Alloys in the form of coil, foils, strips, sheets and circles. We have been one of the major producers in the industry since 1935.

Krishna Metal believes in staying updated with the developments of the industry. We educate ourselves on the plant and machinery that we use. Our vast customer base boats of some of the biggest business groups in India which has helped us to be recognised as one of the major manufacturing house in the copper and copper alloy industry in the country. The strong base is because of our customer-centric production techniques and our superior quality products. We believe in manufacturing products that focus on growth while preserving the environment to the best our ability.

To maintain a customer-centric approach, we follow some parameters:

  • * Maintaining adequate level of stock.

  • * Ensuring the most competitive prices for our valued customers .

  • * Working constantly on improving the quality and reducing the lead time.

  • * Providing flexible terms and conditions as per customer requirement .

MISSION

With the mission of “Customer Satisfaction is the Greatest Profit”, we are into the business that focuses on customer requirements. Since its inception, Krishna Metal Works has grown into a leading name in the industry for manufacturing copper and brass products in various forms. Our focus is to enhance product quality, employee work-life balance, and customer satisfaction. We have a great team that focuses on their individual and company growth to reach our mission. The team upgrades themselves with the ongoing technology.

VISION

We at Krishna Metal Works have been successful in cementing our name as a leading manufacturing in the copper and copper alloy industry. Our vision is to become global leaders in the industry and provide the maximum value and best experience to our clients for their purchase.

Manufacturing Range

With our state-of-the-art infrastructure, we at Krishna Metal Works ensure that our vast range of products is timely delivered to our esteemed customers as per their specifications. Proper parameters are followed to keep the workers safe. The products are tested at various stages of the production process to ensure that the clients attain complete satisfaction by using our products.

Quality policy

Customer satisfaction is our ultimate goal. Various parameters are placed to check the final products to achieve this goal. Before reaching the final product, the team sits to study customer requirements closely. We have a quality management team that works closely with the manufacturing unit for quality control and upgradation. For constant innovation, the team undergoes compulsory training.

Environment Policy

To keep the business growing, we need to stay friendly with nature. We strictly abide by “ No environment is harmed during the production process.” The whole manufacturing procedure of copper and copper alloys in various forms is done with the environmental rules list. We have a team that monitors the production process. Also, we keep the environmental policy in our business meetings to discuss better measures.

About Industry

Krishna Metal Works provides a wide range of Copper, Brass & Phosphor Bronze Sheets, Strips and Foil in a variety of shapes and sizes. Our extensive range offers unmatched flexibility to meet the needs of varied industries. Matching the industry standards, KMW produce a vast range of materials that are not just mechanically sound but they also offer superior performance and superior quality. KMW boasts of a high level of customer satisfaction and has been featured in many prestigious magazines.
